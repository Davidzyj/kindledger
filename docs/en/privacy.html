Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 26, 2026

KindTrace is designed as a private, offline-first app. It helps you record people who helped you and the kindness you want to remember.

Data Collection

KindTrace does not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal data. The app does not include user accounts, analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking in version 1.0.0.

Local Storage

Your records are stored locally on your device. The app does not upload your records to a server.

Network Use

The app does not make background network requests. If you tap the privacy policy or support links, your browser opens the relevant web page.
